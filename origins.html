ORIGINS -- WHAT ALL MATTER IS TETHERED TO.

All tangible matter is tethered to an Origin, whether sapient or inanimate.
Some think of The Origins as immaterial, all-encompassing energies, while others view them
as omnipotent deities, serving and worshipping them as such. All matter can be traced back
to an Origin, down to the molecular level. Sapient beings have been observed
to have "Tethers" to their Origins, acting as a sort of conduit
through which the being receives vitality.
