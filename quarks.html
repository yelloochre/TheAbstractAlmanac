QUARKS -- THE INGREDIENTS FOR MATTER.


Sandstone is made up of sand... but what is sand made up of ? If you could disassemble
an object down to its core components, what you'd end up with is a pile of microscopic
particles called Quarks.

Quarks appear in many variations, each reacting to other variations in unique ways.
These reactions include repelling, consuming, multiplying, and attracting and forming bonds
with other quarks.
When the right combination of Quarks are assembled, matter is formed. From grass to flames,
flesh to bone, caut to air... it all comes down to Quarks.
